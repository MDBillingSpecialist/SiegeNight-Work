description=[i]Every night could be your last.[/i]
description=
description=Siege Night turns your base into a battleground. Every few nights, the dead march on your position from a single direction -- wave after wave, growing stronger as the night goes on. Build your walls. Stock your ammo. Survive until dawn.
description=
description=Built for fortress defense. Solo or multiplayer.
description=
description=[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MDBillingSpecialist/SiegeNight-Work/main/images/divider.png[/img]
description=
description=[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/MDBillingSpecialist/SiegeNight-Work/main/images/banner-features.png[/img]
description=
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Wave Cycles[/b] -- WAVE (dense assault), TRICKLE (stragglers), and BREAK (patch up and reinforce). 3 to 7 waves per night, escalating as midnight approaches.
description=[*] [b]Directional Attacks[/b] -- every siege picks one of 8 compass directions and all zombies come from that angle for the entire night. Different directions each siege force real decisions about where your walls go.
description=[*] [b]Siege Scaling[/b] -- zombie count scales with sieges survived and players online. Early game: 3 waves, ~75-150 zombies. Late game: 6-7 waves, up to 1,500.
description=[*] [b]Per-Group Independent Sieges[/b] -- in MP, groups in different areas each get their own siege with separate waves, direction, kill tracking, and clear condition.
description=[*] [b]Safehouse Anchor Mode[/b] -- zombies target your claimed safehouse instead of your player position. They attack your base even if you leave.
description=[*] [b]Dawn Survival[/b] -- make it to sunrise and your character announces the kill count. The siege panel tracks everything.
description=[*] [b]30+ Sandbox Options[/b] -- tune siege frequency, zombie count, scaling, spawn distance, anchor mode, special zombie rates, mini-horde thresholds, and more. Spread across 3 sandbox pages.
description=[*] [b]Kill Tracking[/b] -- a Siege Night tab in your character info (press H) shows wave/phase, kills this siege, lifetime totals, and full siege history for the last 20 nights.
description=[/list]

description=[*] [b]Breakers[/b] -- construction gear. Built to smash through your barricades and walls.
description=[*] [b]Tanks[/b] -- military outfits, 5x normal health. Two per siege by default.

description=
description=Between siege nights, your activity generates [b]heat[/b]. Generators (+15), gunfire (+10), driving (+8), heavy construction (+5). Heat builds around your location and decays slowly when you go quiet.
description=
description=Push your luck too far and you'll hear: [i]"Something's attracting them from the south..."[/i]
description=
description=Mini-hordes come from the direction of your heat source. An attractor system pulls them toward you. Kill enough and the attractor shuts off -- the rest scatter. Cooldown between triggers and a daily cap so you won't get chain-hit.

description=[h1]Commands[/h1]
description=
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]!siege start[/b] -- Start a siege immediately (admin only in MP)
description=[*] [b]!siege stop[/b] -- End the current siege (admin only in MP)
description=[*] [b]!siege status[/b] -- Show current siege state, kills, and progress
description=[*] [b]!siege next[/b] -- Show when the next siege is scheduled
description=[*] [b]!siege vote[/b] -- Start a vote to trigger a siege (any player)
description=[*] [b]!siege yes[/b] -- Vote yes on an active vote
description=[*] [b]!siege optout / optin[/b] -- Opt out/in of sieges
description=[/list]
description=
description=Vote system needs majority of online players. 60 second window. SP uses Numpad debug keys (Numpad 0 to enable, then 2/7/9/6 for controls).
